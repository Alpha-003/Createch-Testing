A functional and integrated approach to spine health, mobility and wellness.

Welcome to White Spine Health & Wellness. Our team is dedicated to providing you with comprehensive and conservative treatment approach for individuals with chronic or recurrent neck or back pain. With our convenient. South Florida location, telemedicine appointment system, and responsive diagnostic procedures, we are committed to providing individualize, effective care to get back to the pain-free life that you love.

Comprehensive Care

We believe that optimal wellness is best achieved through a comprehensive, multi-disciplinary approach to wellness. This balanced approach to healthcare prevents and heals disease and supports a healthy. vital life style

Predict and Prevent

Our team of dedicated physicians, physical therapist, nutritionist, Registered Nurses and professional support staff meet regularly to discuss our patient's healt care, and how to accelerate each individual's healing pr0cess towards optimal wellness.

Proactive not reactive

We strive to achieve proactive solutions, instead of reacting to and treating only sympotoms through a comprehensive program of regular medical visits (utilizing our state-of-the-art diagnostic tools), sound nutrition, vitamin and supplement support, and physical therapy methods.
